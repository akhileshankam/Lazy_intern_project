About lazyIntern

We're revolutionizing how companies discover and hire student talent by focusing on skills and projects rather than grades and university rankings.

Our Mission

Bridge the gap between talented students and forward-thinking companies by creating a platform where skills matter more than credentials.

Our Vision

A world where every talented student can showcase abilities and land their dream internship—regardless of academic background.

Our Impact

Connecting companies with candidates who prove themselves through real projects and practical skills, not just traditional metrics.

Why We Started lazyIntern

Too many brilliant students with strong coding skills and real projects were overlooked simply because they didn't attend top-tier universities or maintain perfect GPAs.

Meanwhile, companies struggled to find developers who could contribute from day one. The traditional hiring process was failing both sides.

lazyIntern was born from the belief that what you can build matters more than where you studied. We help students showcase real projects and enable companies to discover genuine talent based on actual skills.

Whether you're a student ready to showcase projects or a company seeking real talent, lazyIntern is your platform.
